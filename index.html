About Me

Hi, welcome to my webpage.
I am Rahul Kulkarni, a final-year BS-MS student at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Bhopal, specializing in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science.

My primary research passion lies at the intersection of Autonomous Systems and Robotics. I am focused on the complex challenges of designing, controlling, and planning paths for autonomous marine and aerial drones.
I am also keen to leverage my skills in interdisciplinary research, particularly in developing smart systems for applications in hydrology and disaster mitigation.

I am currently a researcher at the Multi-Robot Autonomy Lab (MOON Lab), where I am completing my MS thesis on the "Design and Control of a Hybrid Cycloidal Propeller Drone." In the summer of 2023, I had the valuable experience of being a SPARK Research Fellow at Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee.
