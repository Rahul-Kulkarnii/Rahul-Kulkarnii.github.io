About Me

Hi, welcome to my webpage. I am Rahul Kulkarni, a final year BS-MS student at the Indian Insitute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Bhopal in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science.

My primary research interest is in Autonomous Systems and Robotics, particularly in Design, Control and Path Planning of Autonomous Drones in the realm of Marine and Aerial robotics. I am also keen in working on interdisciplinary problems and application of Smart Systems in multi-disciplinary research like Hydrology and Disaster Mitigation.

I am currently a researcher (2023 - Present) in Multi Robot Autonomy Lab (MOON Lab) at the dept of EECS in IISER Bhopal, where I am also doing my MS thesis on "Design and Control of Hybrid Cycloidal Propeller Drone". I was also the SPARK Research Fellow (Summer 2023) at the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee.
